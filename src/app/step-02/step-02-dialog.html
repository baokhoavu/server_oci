Welcome to the Online Check-In!

This automated check-in process is designed to save you time and make event check-in easier than ever! Here is what you'll need to do:

  • We recommend using Google Chrome for the smoothest experience.
  • Review your online profile.
  • Enter emergency contact information.
  • Watch our mandatory safety video.
  • See below for Team Fundraising info. If you need more assistance, please call your OneWalk coaches [416] 815-WALK [9255].

Any walker who has not met their individual fundraising minimum by Friday, September 6 will have their eligibility to walk calculated based on their TEAM fundraising total. If your team has not raised enough funds to get you to your $1,500 or $2,000 minimum, you will be required to complete a Delayed Self Pledge in order to walk. Check in with your team captain before completing Online Check-In, or call your OneWalk Coaches.
